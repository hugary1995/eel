HLLCUserObject

Computes free-flow wave speeds on internal sides, useful in HLLC contexts

Overview

This object computes the HLLC left, middle, and right wave-speeds (see CNSFVHLLCBase) from velocity, speed, pressure, density, and specific internal energy element and neighbor material properties (or left and right material properties if you want to think of it that way) on internal faces. These wave speeds can then be used in objects like WaveSpeedVPP
