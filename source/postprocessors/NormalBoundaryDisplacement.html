NormalBoundaryDisplacement

This postprocessor computes the normal displacement on a given set of boundaries.

Description

NormalBoundaryDisplacement computes average or maximum normal displacement on a boundary. It permits modes: average absolute_average max absolute_max which are as follows:

average: where is the surface area, is the normal vector, and is the displacement vector.
