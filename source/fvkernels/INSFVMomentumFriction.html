INSFVMomentumFriction

Implements a basic linear or quadratic friction model as a volumetric force, for example for the X-momentum equation: and for the linear and quadratic models respectively. A linear dependence is expected for laminar flow, while a quadratic dependence is more common for turbulent flow.
