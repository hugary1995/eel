CNSFVMassHLLC

Implements the mass flux portion of the free-flow HLLC discretization.

Overview

This object implements the mass equation inter-cell fluxes for the Harten-Lax-Van Leer-Contact (HLLC) formulation described in CNSFVHLLCBase.
