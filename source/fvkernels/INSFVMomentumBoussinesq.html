INSFVMomentumBoussinesq

This object adds a term to the incompressible Navier Stokes (INS) equations where is the density, is the thermal expansion coefficient, is the gravity vector, is the temperature, and is a reference temperature. The term above introduces the Boussinesq approximation into the INS equations, which allows for modeling natural convection.
